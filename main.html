Plot Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction action film that follows Dominic Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the dangerous art of **extraction**, stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state, when the mind is most vulnerable. Cobb is offered a chance at redemption: his final job is not to steal an idea, but to plant one—a process called **inception**. To succeed, he must assemble a specialized team and descend through multiple layers of a shared dreamscape, all while battling his own tormented past and the projections of his deceased wife, Mal, who haunts his subconscious mind.

The Team (Key Actors & Characters)

The film boasts an incredible ensemble cast, portraying the various specialists needed for the inception job:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb (The Extractor/Inceptor)
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur (The Point Man)
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne (The Architect)
  • Tom Hardy as Eames (The Forger)
  • Ken Watanabe as Mr. Saito (The Tourist/Client)
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer (The Target)
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal (Cobb's Subconscious Projection)
